Privacy Policy for Simple Tabs

Last Updated: Jan 7, 2025

Data Collection and Use

This extension processes certain data locally on your device to enable its functionality, such as switching between recently used tabs. The data includes:

  • Web history: Tab URLs and titles are temporarily processed to manage tab-switching.
  • User activity: Tab switching behavior is tracked locally to provide the most recently used functionality.

Data Storage and Sharing

- No data is transmitted, stored remotely, or shared with any third party.
- All data is processed and stored locally on your device for the sole purpose of providing the core functionality of the extension.
- Data is cleared automatically when the browser is closed or the extension is disabled.

User Rights

As no data is collected beyond local processing, there are no special requests or rights needed regarding data access or deletion.
